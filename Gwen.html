Gwen
Gwen Tennyson originally appeared in Ben 10 as Ben's cousin, it was shown that these two did not like each other but as the show progressed the two started showing a deeper family bond.
Gwen has prowess in three different forms of the Martial Arts and is also good gymnast. Apart from those things, she also found out that she could do magic.
During Alien Force and Ultimate Alien, Gwen's mana and magic has grown much more powerful; which all derive from her alien grandmother Verdona, who is an Anodite.
